<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Abstract: Three Confirmed Methods">

Achieving abstraction requires dedicating your character to one of three specific environmental triggers. You do not need to meet all three conditions simultaneously; maintaining a single condition long enough will initiate the transformation sequence.

| Method | Trigger Condition | Interruption Risk |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Isolation** | Stay away from all other players | Another player walking into your area |
| **Darkness** | Stand in unlit rooms or corridors | Someone turning on the room lights |
| **Proximity** | Remain close to an abstracted player | The transformed player moving away |

<Callout type="tip" title="Speed up the Process">
The fastest way to abstract is to enter a private player room, turn off the light switch, and close the door to combine the Darkness and Isolation methods.
</Callout>

Follow these steps to successfully trigger the abstraction sequence:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Method">
    Pick one of the three routes: finding an isolated spot, sitting in a dark room, or standing next to an already transformed player.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Isolate Your Avatar">
    Move away from the central stage area. If you chose the darkness route, interact with a light switch in a private room to extinguish the lights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain the Condition">
    Stand completely still and do not return to the main group. Moving back into illuminated social zones will immediately reset your progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Transformation">
    Once the threshold is met, your avatar will play the standard abstraction cutscene, morphing into a glitching, multi-eyed dark silhouette.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Resist Abstraction">

If your goal is to survive as a normal player for as long as possible, you must actively counter the environmental triggers that cause characters to abstract. Group coordination and spatial awareness are your best defenses.

| Danger Sign | Immediate Threat | Counter Action |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Quiet Corridors** | High risk of isolation | Return to the main circus floor immediately |
| **Flickering Lights** | Creeping darkness trigger | Find a functional light switch or bright room |
| **Glitching Sounds** | Nearby abstracted player | Run in the opposite direction toward a group |

<Callout type="warning" title="Group Dynamics">
Servers that scatter into individual rooms are highly vulnerable. Establish a central regroup point on the main stage to keep everyone safe.
</Callout>
