<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Three Abstraction Routes">

To complete a successful **experience abstraction walkthrough**, you must first understand how to trigger the transformation. Unlike traditional Roblox horror games, progression here requires you to deliberately succumb to the digital corruption. 

The system tracks three distinct environmental and social variables. Activating any single condition will begin the transformation sequence.

| Route | Primary Trigger | Speed | Safety Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Isolation** | Stay far away from all active players | Medium | High (No player interference) |
| **Darkness** | Remain in unlit rooms or corridors | Fast | Medium (Requires finding light switches) |
| **Proximity** | Stand directly next to an abstracted player | Very Fast | Low (Highly dangerous to nearby survivors) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Transformation Warning">
Returning to a bright area or regrouping with normal players will immediately pause and reverse your abstraction progress. You must commit to your chosen condition until the sequence is fully complete.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Abstraction Walkthrough">

This step-by-step walkthrough details how to successfully transform, survive the transition, and prepare your character for the subsequent late-game encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Corruption Route">
    Choose one of the three core methods: isolation, darkness, or proximity. For beginners, the **Darkness Method** in a private room is the most reliable and easiest to control.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate a Dark Private Room">
    Navigate away from the central stage area. Enter one of the side corridors leading to the player rooms. Walk inside, locate the light switch on the wall, and interact with it to plunge the room into darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain the Trigger Condition">
    Stand still in the center of the dark room. Do not open the door, do not turn the lights back on, and do not let other players enter your space. If another player joins you, the isolation variable resets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Endure the Transformation Sequence">
    Your screen will begin to warp, accompanied by heavy static and chromatic aberration. Your character model will dissolve and rebuild into a towering, glitching polygonal silhouette adorned with colorful neon eyes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Do Not Reset or Leave">
    Once transformed, your gameplay style shifts. You are now an active hazard. To progress to the Caine and Cellar events, you must remain in the server in this mutated state. Do not use the reset character option.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Walkthrough Tip">
If you are struggling to stay isolated, use the dark corners of the transition hallways. Most players gather on the main circus floor, leaving the corridor ends completely empty.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Caine Sequence and Cellar Walkthrough">

Reaching the Cellar is the ultimate objective of the **experience abstraction walkthrough**. This area cannot be accessed through normal map exploration; it is a sequence-only destination tied directly to Caine.

| Event Phase | Player Action | Caine's Role | Destination |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1: Mutated State** | Complete transformation and stay alive | Monitoring server status | Active Circus |
| **Phase 2: The Summoning** | Wait in the main area as an abstracted entity | Spawns to confront the player | Central Stage |
| **Phase 3: The Banished** | Watch the cinematic sequence play out | Casts the mutated player away | The Cellar |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve Full Abstraction">
    Follow the steps in Section 2 to fully transform your avatar. Ensure you do not reset your character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Central Stage">
    Walk your mutated character back to the brightly lit central stage area. This is where special server events are programmed to trigger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wait for Caine's Arrival">
    Remain on the stage. After a brief period, the server will trigger the Caine sequence. Caine will spawn directly in front of you to address your corrupted state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Watch the Cinematic Cutscene">
    Do not disconnect or attempt to move away. Let the entire cinematic animation play out. Caine will interact with your character, initiating the banishment sequence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transition to the Cellar">
    Upon the conclusion of the cutscene, the game will automatically transfer your character from the active circus map into the dark, secluded Cellar. You have successfully completed the route.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Event Disruption">
If another player distracts Caine or if you reset your character mid-cinematic, the sequence will break. If this occurs, you must rejoin a fresh session and restart the walkthrough from step one.
</Callout>
