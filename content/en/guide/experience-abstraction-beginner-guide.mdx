<Section icon={Target} title="Three Methods to Trigger Abstraction">

For many players, the goal isn't just to survive but to experience the transformation itself. There are three confirmed ways to trigger the abstraction sequence in the 2026 build. Each method requires dedication to a specific condition without interruption.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="The Isolation Route">
    - **Method**: Move away from the main server group.
    - **Requirement**: Stay in a room or corridor where no other players are visible.
    - **Difficulty**: Easy, but easily interrupted by wandering players.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Darkness Route">
    - **Method**: Enter an unlit room and switch off available lights.
    - **Requirement**: Remain in total darkness for a prolonged period.
    - **Difficulty**: Medium, requires finding a private, quiet space.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Proximity Route">
    - **Method**: Find a player who has already transformed.
    - **Requirement**: Stay directly beside them for several minutes.
    - **Difficulty**: Hard, as abstracted players often move unpredictably.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Once a condition is met, your screen will begin to glitch, and your avatar will undergo a permanent transformation for that session. You cannot revert to your human form without resetting or joining a new server.

| Method | Time Required | Visual Indicators |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Isolation** | 3-5 Minutes | Fading background noise, subtle screen warping |
| **Darkness** | 2-4 Minutes | Increased static, glowing eyes appearing |
| **Proximity** | 1-3 Minutes | Rapid glitching, neon color shifts |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Combining these methods (e.g., being alone in a dark room) significantly accelerates the transformation process. Be careful if you are trying to stay human!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the Cellar">

Reaching the Cellar is considered the "endgame" of the current Experience Abstraction build. It is not a location you can simply walk into; it requires a specific sequence of events triggered by your abstraction.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Full Abstraction">
    Choose one of the three methods mentioned above. We recommend the **Darkness Route** in a private room for the most consistent results. Wait until your character model completely changes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maintain Your State">
    Do not reset your character. Once abstracted, you must remain in the server. Explore the circus for a few minutes to ensure the game registers your new status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Caine Sequence">
    Wait for the Caine encounter. In the July 2026 update, Caine appears automatically to players who have been abstracted for a specific duration. A cinematic cutscene will begin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Transition">
    During the Caine cutscene, your character will be "handled" by the Ringmaster. Do not disconnect or move during this animation. The scene will fade to black.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Arrival at the Cellar">
    You will wake up in the Cellar. This area is much darker and serves as the final destination for abstracted characters. You can now explore this secret zone until the session ends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
The Cellar is often crowded with other abstracted players. It is the best place to take screenshots and observe the unique animations of the transformed models.
</Callout>
