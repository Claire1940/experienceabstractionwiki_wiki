Once you have successfully transformed, the next step toward the **experience abstraction cellar location** involves the Caine encounter. This is a scripted event that bridges the gap between the circus and the cellar. Many players make the mistake of resetting their character immediately after transforming, which voids the sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maintain Abstracted Form">
    After your avatar changes into the polygonal shard form, do not press 'Esc' and 'Reset'. You must remain in this state to be eligible for the next event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Waiting Period">
    Continue moving around the map or stay in a central area. The Caine sequence usually triggers within a few minutes of a player becoming abstracted, depending on server activity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Cutscene">
    Caine will appear in a flash of light or through a specific animation. The camera will lock, and a short cinematic will play showing Caine's reaction to your "corrupted" state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Cellar Transfer">

  </Step>
