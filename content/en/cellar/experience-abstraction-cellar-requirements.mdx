<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Experience Abstraction Cellar Requirements">

The Cellar is a highly sought-after, sequence-locked location in the Roblox social horror hit, Experience Abstraction (developed by pawlooz). Unlike standard Roblox maps where secret rooms are accessed via hidden doors or invisible walls, reaching the Cellar is entirely progression-based. To unlock this dark zone, you must satisfy the exact **experience abstraction cellar requirements** by completing a full character transformation.

Because the game is inspired by Glitch Productions' *The Amazing Digital Circus* (TADC), the transition mimics the descent of a corrupted character. You cannot walk, glitch, or jump into the Cellar as a normal player. The game engine only loads the Cellar transition once your avatar undergoes a complete state change.

| Requirement Type | Prerequisite Condition | Expected Result |
|---|---|---|
| **State Requirement** | Complete 1 of 3 Abstraction methods | Character enters Abstracted state |
| **Server Requirement** | Do not reset or leave after transforming | Maintains the active event chain |
| **NPC Requirement** | Trigger and watch the Caine sequence | Initiates the cinematic teleport |
| **Location Target** | Successful transition completion | Spawn inside the dark Cellar |

<Callout type="warning" title="Progression Warning">
Resetting your character, respawning, or disconnecting from the server immediately after transforming will break the event loop. If you break the chain, you must restart the entire abstraction process from the beginning.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Route to the Cellar">

To successfully meet the **experience abstraction cellar requirements**, players must follow a strict, linear progression path. The event chain begins with deliberate transformation and ends with the cinematic transition into the Cellar.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Trigger Character Abstraction">
    Select one of the three confirmed abstraction methods: isolate yourself from the group, stay in a completely dark room, or stand next to an already abstracted player. Maintain this state until the transformation cutscene begins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maintain the Abstracted State">
    Once your avatar transforms into the polygonal, multi-eyed abstracted form, do not touch the reset button. You must remain active in the server as an abstracted entity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wait for the Caine Encounter">
    Stay in the playable circus area. Caine will eventually spawn in response to your abstraction. This cinematic sequence is required to bridge your character from the main hall to the hidden depths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Cellar Transition">
    Allow the Caine animation to play in its entirety without exiting the game. Once the sequence concludes, the game will automatically transfer your character directly into the Cellar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
The fastest way to trigger the initial abstraction is by combining methods. Enter a private room, switch off the lights to create darkness, and ensure no other players follow you to trigger the isolation condition.
</Callout>
