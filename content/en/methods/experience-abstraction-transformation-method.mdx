<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Transformation Methods">

To successfully initiate the experience abstraction transformation method, players can choose from three distinct environmental triggers. Each method targets the sanity meter located at the bottom of the user interface.

### Method 1: The Room Lights Technique

This is the safest and most reliable method, especially when playing on a private server. It allows you to control the environment without interference from other players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Your Room">
    Navigate through the main lobby and find the door marked with the arrow. Go upstairs to the residential corridor and locate your designated player room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interact with the Switch">
    Enter your room and locate the light switch. Interact with it to turn off all the lights, plunging the room into darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor Sanity">
    Stand still inside the dark room. Keep your eyes on the sanity meter at the bottom of the screen. It will gradually decrease over the course of three minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Embrace the Transformation">
    Once the sanity meter reaches zero, the transformation will trigger. Dark, glitching tendrils will wrap around your character model, completing the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: The Tower Climb Technique

If you prefer a more active approach, the central tower offers a faster but riskier alternative.

| Step | Action | Sanity Effect | Danger Factor |
|-|-|-|-|
| 1 | Locate the central tower in the main lobby area | Neutral | Low |
| 2 | Climb the internal ladders to reach the summit | Gradual decrease | Medium |
| 3 | Wait at the top platform until sanity depletes | Rapid decrease | High |

### Method 3: The Proximity Technique

This method relies on multiplayer interaction. By standing close to a player who has already undergone the transformation, your sanity will drain rapidly.

<Callout type="tip" title="Proximity Management">
When using the proximity method, ensure your health does not deplete before your sanity. If your health reaches zero first, your sanity meter will reset, forcing you to restart the process. Maintain a safe distance to balance sanity drain and health loss.
</Callout>

Remaining in the abstracted form is not permanent. The game features a built-in balance mechanic monitored by Caine.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Cellar Trap">
If you maintain the abstracted transformation for approximately five minutes, a sudden event will trigger. Caine will appear and banish you to the cellar. Once inside this eerie, glitching space, you are trapped indefinitely. There are no escape routes or interactive objects.
</Callout>

| Location | Escape Method | Interaction Options | Time Limit |
|-|-|-|-|

To return to the main game loop from the cellar, you must open the game menu and select the respawn option. This resets your character to the normal state and places you back in the lobby.
