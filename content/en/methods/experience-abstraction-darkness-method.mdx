<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Darkness Abstraction Tutorial">

To successfully execute the **experience abstraction darkness method**, you must follow a specific sequence of actions. The game tracks your "exposure" to darkness, and any flicker of light from a hallway or another player's flashlight can reset your progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Private Room">
    Move away from the **Main Circus Floor** and enter one of the side hallways. Look for a private room with a functional light switch. These are typically located behind the colorful doors lining the corridors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Kill the Lights">
    Interact with the light switch to turn off all illumination in the room. The environment should be completely dark, save for any neon accents on the walls or your own UI.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Total Stillness">
    Stand in the center of the dark room. Moving toward the door or window may expose your character to ambient light from the hallway, which pauses the **experience abstraction darkness method** timer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Visual Distortions">
    After roughly 60-90 seconds, you will notice sharp polygonal shards beginning to form around your avatar. Your eyes will start to glow with neon magenta, cyan, and lime hues.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Complete the Sequence">
    The screen will briefly glitch, and your character will fully transform. You are now an abstracted entity, capable of triggering the next phase of the game: the **Caine Encounter**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategy: Resisting the Darkness">

Not every player wants to abstract. If your goal is to survive as long as possible, you must actively counter the **experience abstraction darkness method**. Survival in this Roblox experience is a game of social coordination and light management.

### Group Cohesion
The most effective way to resist the dark is to stay with the **Main Group**. The game's engine checks for proximity to other "human" players. As long as you are within a few studs of another player, the abstraction timer for darkness and isolation is significantly slowed.

### Light Management
- **Stay in the Center**: The main circus floor is the most consistently lit area.
- **Avoid Private Rooms**: Entering a room alone is an invitation for the darkness mechanic to kick in.
- **Watch the Lights**: If you see a player turning off light switches, leave the area immediately.
