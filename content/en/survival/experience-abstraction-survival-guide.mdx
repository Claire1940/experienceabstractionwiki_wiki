<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity Mechanics and Abstraction States">

Sanity is the defining mechanic of the game. Your current mental state is tracked by a dedicated sanity meter located at the bottom of the user interface. When this meter is full, you remain in your normal character form, allowing you to explore, customize your bedroom, and chat with other players. However, specific environmental triggers will cause this meter to drain.

Once the sanity meter reaches zero, your character undergoes a dramatic transformation. You lose your human appearance and become covered in a glitching, multi-eyed dark void. This state grants you offensive capabilities but limits your interaction options.

| State | Movement | Combat Capability | Sanity Level | Escape Method |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normal | Standard speed | Defensive only | 1% to 100% | Standard gameplay |
| Abstracted | Glitched movement | Damage other players | 0% (Depleted) | Respawn menu |

Understanding the differences between these two states is crucial for planning your survival strategy. While the normal state is ideal for exploration and customization, the abstracted state shifts your role to a predator, allowing you to hunt down remaining players in the server.

| Method | Location | Sanity Drain Rate | Time Required | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dark Room | Personal Bedroom | Moderate | 3 minutes | Low |
| Tower Climb | Central Tower Top | High | 2 minutes | Medium |
| Glitched Friend | Near Abstracted Player | Very High | 1 minute | High |

<Callout type="tip" title="Sanity Recovery Tip">
If you want to reset your sanity meter without completing a full transformation, you can allow your health to run out before the sanity meter depletes entirely. Resetting your health will restore your sanity meter to maximum.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Trigger Abstraction">

While survival is the main goal, many players actively seek to experience the abstraction transformation. Becoming abstracted unlocks new gameplay options and is required to access the hidden cellar area. There are three distinct pathways to achieving this state.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Your Personal Room">
    Head upstairs through the door marked with the arrow. Find your designated bedroom. Use the light switch near the door to turn off all lights, plunging the room into complete darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Wait for Sanity Depletion">
    Stand still in the dark room. Monitor the sanity meter at the bottom of your screen. It will take approximately three minutes of standing in darkness for the meter to empty completely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Climb the Central Tower">
    Alternatively, leave the rooms and scale the tall tower structure in the center of the map. Standing at the peak of the tower exposes you to the void, draining your sanity rapidly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Stand Near an Abstracted Player">
    The fastest method involves finding another player who has already transformed. Standing directly adjacent to an abstracted player will drain your sanity meter in about one minute.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inescapable Cellar Warning">
Once you enter the cellar after five minutes of abstraction, you are trapped in a dark, glitchy maze. There are no doors, switches, or events that allow you to escape this zone. The only way to return to the main circus area is to open the settings menu and select the respawn option.
</Callout>

</Section>
