Becoming abstracted is the primary gameplay hook in Experience Abstraction. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Dark Room Method">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Tower Climb Method">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Proximity Method">
    Stand directly next to a player who has already undergone the abstraction transformation. The dark aura radiating from them will drain your sanity meter rapidly.
  </Step>
