<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Isolation Route">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Darkness Route">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Proximity Route">
    Find a player who has already undergone abstraction. By standing near them, you "catch" the effect. This social spread of abstraction is a key mechanic in higher-population servers.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for Non-Abstracted Players">

If your goal is to survive as long as possible without succumbing to the **experience abstraction light and darkness** effects, you must adopt a defensive playstyle. Survival in Experience Abstraction is a social effort; lone wolves are the first to transform.

**Key Survival Tactics:**
- **The Buddy System**: Never enter a room or hallway alone. Always ensure at least two other players are within your line of sight.
- **Light Management**: If you enter a room where the lights are off, immediately find the switch or leave.
- **Abstracted Avoidance**: Abstracted players are "contagious." If you see a glitching silhouette, maintain a distance of at least 10 studs.
- **Regrouping**: If the server population begins to scatter, return to the Main Circus Floor and call for others to join you.

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning Signs">
If your screen begins to desaturate or you hear low-frequency humming, you are currently undergoing abstraction. Move toward a light source and other players immediately to reverse the effect.
</Callout>
